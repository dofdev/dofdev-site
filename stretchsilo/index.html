stretch silo

A stretch cursor targeting system for a 360 missile defense game.

Six cities are being attacked by an endless hail of ballistic missiles, some of which split like multiple independently targetable reentry vehicles. New aggressors are introduced in later stages: bomber planes who fly overhead, kamikazes that can evade a less-than-perfectly targeted missile and aliens that loop around the island launching missiles of their own. As a regional commander of three anti-missile batteries, you must utilize your stretch-cursor and time-shift drive to counter the onslaught and defend your cities from being destroyed.
